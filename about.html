FAQ

How does moonfi work?
We convert your deposits on moonfi into USDC, a fully-collateralized stablecoin pegged to the value of the US dollar backed by crypto giants Coinbase and Circle. This yield is generated by putting USDC into high-yield decentralized finance protocols, like AAVE and Uniswap, which generate the yield for our users!
Are my moonfi deposits stable?
All holdings are converted to USDC, which is always redeemable for 1 US dollar. This makes USDC a relatively secure asset because it has tangible, redeemable value!
How are the yields generated?
We generate yield through decentralized finance protocols which have a higher yield than traditional finance due to high demand for USDC and the lack of a middleman taking large cuts of your interest.
